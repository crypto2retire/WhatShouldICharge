Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information (Business Users): When you create an account, we collect your email address, password (stored as a bcrypt hash), company name, city, and state. We also store your subscription status and pricing configuration.

Customer Information (End Customers): When customers use the estimation tool, we collect the name, email address, and phone number they provide. This information is encrypted at rest and used solely to facilitate communication between the customer and the junk removal service provider.

Uploaded Photos: Photos uploaded for estimation are processed by AI to identify items and calculate volumes. Photos are temporarily stored during processing and may be retained for the purpose of generating estimate records.

Usage Data: We collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps for security, rate limiting, and service improvement purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI-assisted junk removal estimates
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Communicate with you about your account or service updates
  • Share customer contact information with the relevant junk removal service provider (the business whose estimate page the customer used)
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and enforce rate limits
  • Improve the accuracy of our estimation algorithms

3. How We Share Your Information

With Junk Removal Service Providers: Customer contact information and estimate details are shared with the business that operates the specific estimate page the customer used. This is the primary purpose of collecting customer data.

Third-Party Service Providers: We use the following services to operate the platform:

  • Stripe — payment processing (subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — photo analysis and estimation (photos are processed via API and subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy)
  • SendGrid — email delivery
  • DigitalOcean — image storage and hosting
  • Railway — application hosting

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of customer PII (name, email, phone) at rest using Fernet symmetric encryption
  • Bcrypt hashing for passwords
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • HTTP-only session cookies with secure flags
  • CSRF protection on all state-changing requests
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Content Security Policy headers

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Estimate records are retained according to your subscription tier (30-day history for Starter, unlimited for Professional and Business). Customer contact information associated with estimates is retained for the same period. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Export your estimate data

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@whatshouldicharge.app.

7. Cookies

We use HTTP-only session cookies to maintain your login state. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. The only cookie we set is a session token for authentication purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed to: support@whatshouldicharge.app
