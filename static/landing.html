The Problem

Pricing junk removal jobs is hard.
It doesn't have to be.

Every estimate carries risk. Quote too high, you lose the job. Quote too low, you work for nothing. There's a better way.

💸

Quoting too low?

You're leaving real money on the table. Underestimating a hoarder situation or a load with heavy appliances can cost you hundreds per job.

📉

Quoting too high?

Customers compare prices. If you're over-quoting out of caution, you're handing jobs to competitors who are pricing more confidently.

🚗

Wasting time on site visits?

Driving out just to give a ballpark number burns time, fuel, and money. Most jobs can be estimated accurately from photos alone.
