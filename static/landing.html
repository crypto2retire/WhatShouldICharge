The Problem

Pricing junk removal jobs is hard.
It doesn't have to be.

Every estimate carries risk. Quote too high, you lose the job. Quote too low, you work for nothing. There's a better way.

💸

Quoting too low?

You're leaving real money on the table. Underestimating a hoarder situation or a load with heavy appliances can cost you hundreds per job.

📉

Quoting too high?

Customers compare prices. If you're over-quoting out of caution, you're handing jobs to competitors who are pricing more confidently.

🚗

Wasting time on site visits?

Driving out just to give a ballpark number burns time, fuel, and money. Most jobs can be estimated accurately from photos alone.

How the AI Works

Spatial reasoning, not guesswork

Our AI doesn't claim to see through piles. Here's what it actually does.

📏

Reference Library

86+ common junk removal items with real L×W×H dimensions. The AI recognizes these items in your photos and uses their known size as measuring references.

📐

Spatial Calibration

The AI uses known-size objects as rulers — a couch at 84×36×34 inches, a door at 80×32 inches, even electrical outlets and wall studs at 16" spacing. More reference points visible = better calibration.

⚙️

Your Review, Your Call

Every estimate shows which items were detected. You can uncheck items that stay, adjust the scope, and the price recalculates instantly. The AI assists — you decide.
